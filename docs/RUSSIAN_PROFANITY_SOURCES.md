# Russian profanity dictionaries

The obscene vocabulary is curated from public Russian profanity-dictionary material, including [Russki-mat.net](https://www.russki-mat.net/e/mat_slovar.htm) and the [Russian Dictionaries idiomatics reference](https://www.slovari.ru/default.aspx?p=349). The literary-abuse list is deliberately maintained separately and produces a lower-risk signal.

The lists contain normalized lemmas and common forms. They are configuration data, not executable code; additions require a policy review and tests.
